Dr. R. Chidambaram

Rajagopala Chidambaram is an Indian Physicist who is known for his integral role in India's nuclear weapons program. He coordinated test preparation for the Pokhran-I (1975) and Pokhran-II (1998). He was the first to introduce automation for data collection with neutron diffractometers. He was also instrumental in introducing crystallographic computing in India.

Manoj Dharmarajan

Manoj Dharmarajan is an officer of the Central Services Group A of the Govt of India and has served as Director in Department of Telecommunications in New Delhi. A seasoned investor and an investment guru, he has delivered lectures on the art of investing in various parts of the country and he has been to 41 countries around the world.

Atul Gurtu

Atul Gurtu is a leading high energy physicist in India. He has been involved in experimental particle physics research, a field that attempts to delve into the nature of the fundamental constituents of matter and the forces between them. His group at the Tata Inst was part of large experimental collaborations based at CERN, the European Laboratory for Particle Physics.
